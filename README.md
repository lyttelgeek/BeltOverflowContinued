# Belt Overflow Continued

Causes all end-belts to dump their items on the ground. Whenever items on a belt are unable to proceed ahead smoothly, they will fall off the belt onto a nearby tile. That tile might be empty, or it could be onto another belt, wreaking havoc on your production line.

Items can fall off the end of a series of belts, splitters, and underground belts, at side loading locations, at splitters being used to combine two belts; anywhere a belt ends or backs up.

 * Updated from the original **Belt Overflow** mod made by Clarence "Sparr" Risher and GopherAtl

---

## Dealing with overflowing belts:
 * Ensure demand exceeds supply, more assemblers than furnaces
 * Put chests at overflow points
 * Return unused materials back to the main bus
 * Pull items directly from your main bus
 * Use logisitic bots
 * Use belt loops everywhere, so inserters won't put items on full belts
 * Use direct insertion between buildings, furnace to assembler to assembler
 * Use circuits and/or smart inserters to avoid over-production

## Minimising damage from overflow:
 * Ensure no belt terminates near another belt
 * Use splitter filters or smart inserters to filter trash from belts before they reach your assemblers
 
 ---

## Compatibility

- Factorio 2.0+
- Space age compatible (not required, space platforms are excluded)
